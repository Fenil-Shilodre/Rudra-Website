Who We Are

Rudra Industries is a trusted manufacturer of FIBC bags, paper bags, and woven sacks, serving industries across India and beyond. Founded with a vision to deliver packaging solutions that combine strength, reliability, and precision, we have grown into a full-scale industrial packaging manufacturer.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility is equipped with modern machinery and operated by a skilled team committed to maintaining the highest quality standards at every stage of production.
