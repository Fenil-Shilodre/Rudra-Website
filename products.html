Plastic Liner Bags

PP/HDPE Woven Sack Bags

Polypropylene and HDPE woven sacks are among the most widely used industrial packaging solutions. Lightweight yet strong, they are ideal for grains, fertilizers, sand, and a wide range of industrial materials.

  • Light weight, Easy Transport & Cost Effective
  • Gusseted, Coated or Uncoated options
  • High Tensile Strength and Durability
  • No Seepages in Poly Lined or Laminated Bags
  • 2/3/4 colour flexo, Multi colour & BOPP printing

FIBC Bags (Jumbo Bags)

Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers (FIBC), commonly known as jumbo bags or bulk bags, are large industrial containers designed for storing and transporting dry, flowable products. Our FIBC bags are manufactured using high-tenacity polypropylene woven fabric.

  • Safe Working Load (SWL): up to 1500 kg
  • MOC: PP UV Stabilized
  • Safety Factor: 5:1 or 6:1 as per requirements
  • PE Liner: 40-125 Micron Thickness
  • Quality: Made as per Industry Standard
  • Size: As per Requirements

Laminated Paper Bags
