FIBC Bags (Jumbo Bags)

Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers (FIBC), commonly known as jumbo bags or bulk bags, are large industrial containers designed for storing and transporting dry, flowable products. Our FIBC bags are manufactured using high-tenacity polypropylene woven fabric.

  • Safe Working Load (SWL): up to 1500 kg
  • MOC: PP UV Stabilized
  • Safety Factor: 5:1 or 6:1 as per requirements
  • PE Liner: 40-125 Micron Thickness
  • Quality: Made as per Industry Standard
  • Size: As per Requirements

Specifications

Material
PP Woven Fabric (UV Stabilized)
Fabric Specs
700 – 2000 Denier; 160 – 240 GSM Fabric; Flat / Circular woven
Construction
U-Panel, 4-Panel, Circular (Tubular), Baffle, or Circular Cross Corner
Load & Safety
SWL: 500 kg – 2000 kg; Safety Factor (SF): 5:1 or 6:1
Lifting
1 / 2 Loop, 4 Corner Loops, or Cross Corner Loops
Coating/Liner
Uncoated/Coated (15–30 GSM); LDPE/LLDPE/Form-fit Liner
Printing
Flexographic printing (1 – 3 colours); Single or double side
Optional Features
Baffle, Anti-static, Document pouch, Tamper-proof stitching
