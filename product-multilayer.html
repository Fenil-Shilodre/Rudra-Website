High Strength

Multi-layer Paper Bags

Multi-ply paper bags offer superior strength and protection for sensitive or heavy materials. Each layer adds structural integrity, making these bags ideal for cement, chemicals, and construction materials.

  • Made from High strength sack kraft paper
  • Available with LD/HM Liner, Metallic/Aluminum Foil
  • Flat & Gusseted Tubes as per Requirement
  • All types of Printing & Marking Options
  • Size: Customized as per Requirement
