Plastic Liner Bags

Polyethylene liner bags provide an essential moisture and contamination barrier inside outer packaging. Used as inner liners for FIBC bags, woven sacks, and paper bags to protect contents from humidity and external elements.

  • Complete range of Liner bags including LDPE Rolls
  • Available Type: Liner Bag & Gusseted Liner Bag
  • Size: As per your Requirements
  • Wear and Tear resistant and leakage proof

Specifications

Material
LDPE / LLDPE (Virgin, Food, or Industrial Grade)
Colour & Odor
Transparent, Milky White, or Custom; Odourless (Food-grade)
Film Properties
40 – 150 Microns (µm) Thickness; Blown Film type
Construction
Tube (Cylindrical), Gusseted, or Flat Sheet; Loose or Heat-sealed
Dimensions
Custom sizes available as per bag requirements
Performance
Moisture barrier, Dust protection, Tear & Chemical resistance, Heat-sealing
